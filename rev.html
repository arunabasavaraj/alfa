Career opportunities:

The Business program content prepares students for employment in a variety of roles in this growing industry. Graduates will find challenging employment as:

Administrative Generalist
Marketing Assistant

Students will also have the benefits:

Receiving a Diploma from a well-recognized institution in the Province of Ontario;
Eligibility for up to three-year post-graduation work permit;
Experience Toronto’s multicultural environment;
Easy access to public transportation, restaurants and banking;
Affordable tuition fees and convenient payment options;
Great work opportunities in Toronto;
Alpha College is also a registered education provider through Alpha College of Business and Technology with the Ministry Colleges and Universities, Private Career Colleges Branch. Alpha College’s Private Career College was launched in 2020 to offer quality training to domestic and international students in their desired discipline.
